The CORE System

One system that makes your practice easy to find and trust.

CORE is how we get your practice recognized by Google, Maps, and AI tools. Four parts, in plain terms. Pick one to see the problem it solves and what we actually do.

AI will not recommend a practice it cannot verify.

Before Google, Maps, ChatGPT, or Gemini trusts your practice, it needs consistent proof that you are real, credentialed, and connected across the web.

The problem

Search systems compare your website, business profile, listings, and credentials. Mixed signals make you harder to trust.

  • Name, location, and services do not match everywhere.
  • Credentials and proof are hard to find.
  • Your Google presence is disconnected from the website.

What we do

We give Google and AI one consistent foundation that confirms who you are, where you work, and why you are legitimate.

  • Website, Google Business Profile, and listings aligned.
  • Credentials and provider bios clarified.
  • Public proof organized into a stronger footprint.
See Credibility in detail →

People cannot find a service Google does not understand.

Your site may be accurate and still be unclear. Optimization makes your services, specialties, and locations easy for both people and search tools to understand.

The problem

Vague pages make it harder for Google, Maps, and AI to match you with the right client need.

  • Important services are buried in general copy.
  • Locations and telehealth areas are unclear.
  • Titles, headings, and FAQs do not explain the practice.

What we do

We turn unclear pages into a clean structure that says what you treat, who you help, and how to reach you.

  • Service, location, bio, and FAQ pages clarified.
  • Headings, links, and search labels improved.
  • Booking paths made easier to follow.
See Optimization in detail →

Trust needs to exist outside your own website.

Google and AI look for outside signals that confirm your practice is known, relevant, and connected to real professional context.

The problem

If all your credibility lives on your own site, search and AI have fewer independent signals to trust.

  • Partner proof and endorsements are scattered or missing.
  • Public mentions and profiles are disconnected.
  • Video and educational proof are not tied to the practice.

What we do

We connect the public proof around your practice, without social posting or review management.

  • Partner proof, citations, and mentions connected.
  • Approved proof assets and profile links organized.
  • YouTube and Quora support within clear boundaries.
See Reputation in detail →

Visibility only matters if the right client can take action.

Once someone finds you, the page needs to make the next step obvious, comfortable, and measurable.

The problem

A practice can be visible and still lose good-fit clients if the contact path is vague or hard to trust.

  • Calls to action are inconsistent or hard to find.
  • Contact and booking flows create friction.
  • Reporting does not connect visibility to inquiries.

What we do

We improve the actions, page flow, and reporting that turn visibility into real conversations.

  • Calls to action and contact pages clarified.
  • Booking and inquiry routes tightened.
  • Reporting focused on what changed and what to improve.
