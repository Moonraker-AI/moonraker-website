# Moonraker Website Product Context

## Register

brand

## Product Purpose

We help therapy practices build durable entity visibility across Google Maps, organic search, Gemini, ChatGPT, AI Overviews, and other AI-assisted discovery surfaces.

The public website should explain the CORE Marketing System, establish trust with therapists and practice owners, show proof from client-facing systems, and move qualified practices toward a strategy call.

## Primary Users

- Solo therapists who need more of the right clients and do not want a generic agency.
- Group practice owners who want clearer local search and AI visibility.
- Therapist educators, consultants, and training organizations evaluating Moonraker as a referral partner.
- Existing prospects who have seen a proposal, report, audit, or strategy call page and need public proof.

## Audience State

Visitors are often skeptical, busy, and clinically minded. They may be reviewing the site between sessions, after hours, or while comparing several marketing providers. They want evidence, specificity, and a sense that we understand ethical constraints around mental health marketing.

## Brand Position

We are not a generic SEO shop or a social media marketing agency. We are an entity visibility and implementation partner for therapy practices. The brand should feel precise, calm, practical, and technically current without becoming AI hype.

## Core Offer

The CORE Marketing System is the primary public framework.

- Credibility: prove your practice exists and is grounded to the real world.
- Optimization: teach Google and AI what your practice treats, where it works, and why it is relevant.
- Reputation: build ethical, distributed trust signals through endorsement collection forms, partner proof, citations, press releases, NEO images, Quora Entity Veracity Blocks, YouTube optimization, and active-profile interlinks.
- Engagement: convert visibility into booked consultations through clearer calls to action, booking path clarity, contact page structure, attribution, reporting, and better page structure.

## Funnel

- Phase 0: Entity Health Check. A pre-engagement diagnostic that identifies whether a practice has enough foundation for Moonraker to help and where the gaps are.
- Phase 1: 90-day foundation engagement. Audit, remediation, Cloudflare/R2 site migration or optimization path, technical SEO, schema, page-by-page optimization, citations, Google Business Profile optimization, Entity Veracity Hub, bio/location/service/supporting pages, endorsement collection forms, client-owned YouTube optimization after manager access, and Quora setup/access for future Entity Veracity Blocks.
- Phase 2: ongoing CORE Marketing Campaign. Continued site optimization, press releases, NEO image distribution, LiveDrive, Quora Entity Veracity Block publishing, YouTube channel maintenance and optimization, active-profile interlinking, monthly reporting, recommendations, and the annual-plan-only Performance Guarantee.

## Proof Sources

- Google Partner badge.
- Google rating and selected public feedback.
- Partner logos from therapist educators and practice-building organizations.
- Client testimonials and headshots.
- Client-facing surfaces in Client HQ: proposals, CORE diagnosis, reports, onboarding, progress, campaign summaries, and results pages.
- Process specificity: schema, Google Business Profile, geogrids, service pages, citations, press releases, Entity Veracity Hub, Quora Entity Veracity Blocks, YouTube optimization, ChatGPT/Gemini visibility, and Google Search Console.

## Tone

Plainspoken, specific, grounded, expert.

Use:

- "Here is what AI needs to trust your practice."
- "No vanity metrics."
- "No social posting."
- "No generic SEO package."
- "Built for therapy practices."
- "A practical plan, not a sales performance."

Avoid:

- Generic AI transformation claims.
- Wellness softness.
- Aggressive agency language.
- Hype around ranking guarantees.
- Dense technical copy before the visitor knows why it matters.
- Social media management framing.
- Claims that Moonraker creates client YouTube channels, publishes endorsements, runs paid ads, manages GBP posts or review replies, or provides HIPAA, legal, or compliance consulting.

## Anti References

- Generic green SaaS landing pages.
- Cyber or dark AI aesthetics.
- SEO agency pages with vague "rank higher" promises.
- Endless identical icon cards.
- Big-number hero metrics without context.
- Overly soft therapy/wellness palettes.

## Strategic Principles

- Preserve the shipped Moonraker visual system from Client HQ: dark navy bars, light reading surfaces, green CTAs and state highlights, Outfit headings, Inter body.
- Make proof visible early. Claims should sit near screenshots, logos, badges, selected feedback, named deliverables, or specific mechanisms.
- Treat CORE as the organizing operating system for the site.
- Keep green meaningful. It marks action, signal, progress, and trust.
- Use navy for framing, authority, and end-of-flow conversion bands.
- Prefer diagnosis and evidence over broad promise copy.
