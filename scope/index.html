Working boundaries

What Moonraker does and does not do.

Good marketing should be clear before you sign anything. Here is the simple version of what we handle and what we leave outside the work.

Inside scope

  • Free strategy call and a clear starting-point review.
  • Website cleanup, service-page improvements, and helpful FAQs.
  • Local listings, Google Business Profile improvements, and consistent practice details.
  • YouTube improvements after you give manager access.
  • Approved Quora proof content using client-created access.
  • Reporting, recommendations, and clearer paths to book a consultation.

Outside scope

  • Creating or managing social profiles for Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, or X.
  • Posting to social media, Google Business Profile feeds, or Quora outside approved proof content.
  • Creating client YouTube channels.
  • Publishing endorsements or running review-generation campaigns.
  • Paid ads, ad management, or ad creative.
  • HIPAA, legal, or compliance consulting.
FAQ

Questions we should answer before a call.

Do you post on social media?

No. Moonraker does not create, manage, or post to Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, X, or Google Business Profile feeds. If you already use a practice-facing profile, we may connect it where it helps Google and AI tools understand your practice.

Do you create YouTube channels?

No. The YouTube channel belongs to you. Once you give Moonraker manager access, we can improve the channel so it supports search and AI visibility.

Do you require Google Workspace?

No. A paid domain email account can help your practice look more established, but Google Workspace is recommended, not required. Setting up Workspace itself is not part of our core work.

What is Quora used for?

You create the Quora account or Space. Moonraker uses access only for approved proof content that helps confirm your practice. We do not use it for general posting.

Do you manage Google Business Profile posts or review replies?

No. We can improve the Google Business Profile as a search and Maps asset, but feed posting and review replies stay outside our core work.

Do you run paid ads?

No. Moonraker focuses on long-term visibility in search, Maps, and AI tools, plus reporting and clearer paths from discovery to consultation.

Do you provide HIPAA, legal, or compliance consulting?

No. Moonraker can make practical implementation recommendations, but legal, HIPAA, and compliance decisions should be handled by the practice and its qualified advisors.
