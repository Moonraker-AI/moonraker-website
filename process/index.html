Timeline

The first 90 days are focused on the basics.

Before we ask you to commit to ongoing work, we want the core pieces in place. That makes the next phase more useful and easier to measure.

Phase 0

Entity Health Check

We look at your website, Google profile, local listings, service pages, trust points, and booking path. You get a clear read on where things stand.

Days 1-30

Audit and cleanup plan

We find the biggest issues, set priorities, confirm access, and map the work needed to make your practice easier to find and understand.

Days 31-60

Website and listing foundation

We improve important pages, service content, Google Business Profile, local listings, FAQs, and the proof visitors need before they contact you.

Days 61-90

Proof and reporting baseline

We connect the larger proof system, optimize YouTube after access is granted, prepare Quora for future proof blocks, and set the first reporting baseline.

Ongoing

CORE Marketing Campaign

We continue improving the website, publishing proof assets, maintaining YouTube, adding Quora proof blocks, reporting progress, and recommending next steps.

Working model

You own the accounts. We do the work inside them.

Client owns

  • Domain and website access
  • Google Business Profile
  • YouTube channel
  • Quora account or Space
  • Practice credentials and approvals

Moonraker builds

  • Website cleanup and search basics
  • Clear service and location pages
  • Local listings and consistency
  • Practice proof hub
  • Reports and recommendations

Outside core scope

  • Social media posting
  • Paid ads
  • GBP posts and review replies
  • YouTube channel creation
  • HIPAA, legal, or compliance consulting
