# Moonraker Website Sitemap Plan

## Guiding Principle

The public site should feel like a diagnostic and implementation system for therapy-practice entity visibility, not a generic SEO agency site. Keep the current homepage hero intact while building the surrounding architecture into a clearer conversion path.

Primary conversion: book a free Entity Health Check.

Secondary conversion: help referral partners and qualified prospects understand the CORE system, scope boundaries, proof, and fit before booking.

## Audience and Reading Level

The primary audience is mental health therapists and practice owners, mostly women in the United States and Canada, often 30-55 years old. Many are skeptical of marketing, busy between sessions, and uncomfortable with technical language.

Write at roughly an 8th grade reading level while keeping the tone elegant, responsible, and expert.

Prefer:

- "online footprint" over "entity footprint"
- "website cleanup" over "technical remediation"
- "search-friendly page labels" over "schema"
- "proof hub" over "Entity Veracity Hub" in public-facing copy
- "Quora proof blocks" over "Quora EVBs"
- "where new clients came from" over "attribution"
- "Google Business Profile" over "GBP"

Technical terms can still exist in proposals, reports, or deeper documentation, but public pages should explain the value first in plain language.

## Site Map

### 1. Home

Path: `/`

Purpose:

Position Moonraker as the entity visibility and implementation partner for therapy practices. Preserve the current homepage hero. The rest of the page should move visitors through proof, CORE, fit, funnel, and Entity Health Check conversion.

Primary CTA:

Book a free Entity Health Check.

Secondary CTA:

Explore the CORE System.

Required sections:

- Existing hero, unchanged.
- Partner and badge proof.
- "AI will not recommend what it cannot verify" diagnostic framing.
- CORE overview.
- Proof table with search, local, AI, and attribution surfaces.
- Testimonials and partner proof.
- Fit / not-fit boundaries.
- Phase 0, Phase 1, Phase 2 engagement path.
- Final Entity Health Check CTA.

Notes:

- Keep "No social posting. No vanity metrics. No generic SEO package."
- Do not imply social media management, paid ads, YouTube channel creation, review generation, GBP posting, or compliance consulting.

### 2. Entity Health Check

Future alias:

`/entity-health-check`

Purpose:

Turn the old strategy call into a useful pre-engagement diagnostic. This should feel clear-eyed and helpful, not salesy.

Required sections:

- What the visitor gets from the call.

- Conversion path: homepage hero, CTAs, contact structure, booking flow, attribution.

Conversion angle:

"Find out whether Google and AI can currently trust your practice."
