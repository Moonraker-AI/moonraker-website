Credibility

Before Google or AI trusts your content, you need to prove your practice is grounded to the real world. This is the foundation everything else builds on.

Millions of new pages appear online every day, most of them generated by bots and AI. Before Gemini, ChatGPT, or Google will recommend your practice, they need proof that you're real, that you're located where you say you are, and that there's genuine human activity behind your website. We establish that proof across six key areas.

✉

Google Workspace

Verified email, verified practice

Bots never pay for email. By using a paid Google Workspace account tied to your practice domain, you demonstrate real human activity and legitimate business operations behind your website.

If you're already on Workspace, we create a dedicated marketing account (like marketing@yourpractice.com) to manage your campaign. If you use another email provider, we can start with a free Gmail account for social profiles while still configuring the DNS records that matter most.

🔒

DNS Records (DKIM and DMARC)

Cryptographic proof of domain ownership

DKIM and DMARC records create a security handshake with Google, proving that you own and control your domain. They prevent email spoofing and serve as a strong signal that your website is tied to an active, legitimate practice.

These records can be configured regardless of which email provider you use. They're invisible to your visitors but highly visible to AI systems evaluating your trustworthiness.

📍

Directory Listings and Citations

Consistent identity across the web

We build 15 directory citations plus all 5 major data aggregators: Data Axle, Neustar, YP Network, Foursquare, and GPS Network. The actual reach is hundreds to thousands of listings, including voice search assistants and in-car navigation systems.

These establish consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) information across the web, helping Google verify that the location and contact details on your website are accurate and trustworthy.

What this covers

  • 15 manually built citations through our citation management platform
  • 5 major data aggregators for maximum distribution
  • Consistent NAP across all listings
  • Regional directory variants for Canadian, UK, Australian, NZ, and South African practices
🌊

Interconnected Social Profiles (Rising Tide Strategy)

A real human fingerprint across 9 platforms

Google actively crawls social platforms and understands their connection to a practice. We build and connect profiles across up to 9 platforms, creating an interconnected web of verified social presence that bots simply cannot fake.

Platforms we connect

  • YouTube Channel with a keyword-optimized playlist
  • LinkedIn Business Page
  • Facebook Business Page
  • Quora Space
  • Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, and X Business Profiles

Up to 7 of these are added directly to your Google Business Profile. The remaining platform URLs, along with your playlist and other identifiers, are cross-referenced in your YouTube About section. We always check your preferences before adding platforms, as some practitioners prefer to skip certain networks for personal reasons.

🛡

Entity Veracity Hub

Cryptographic grounding for your practice entity

Our proprietary platform launches the Rising Tide social strategy and connects several cryptographic data points that ground your practice entity to the real world. This includes a DID (Decentralized Identifier), a Google Earth KML file tied to your practice location, your Quora Space, and other verifiable signals.

Think of it as a central hub that ties together all the credibility signals we've built, giving AI systems one clear, verifiable picture of who you are and where you practice.

Why does this matter for AI visibility?

When someone asks Gemini, ChatGPT, or Google AI for a therapist recommendation, the AI doesn't just look at your website. It cross-references dozens of data sources to verify you're real before it puts its reputation on the line by recommending you. Every signal we build in the Credibility phase is another data point confirming: "Yes, this practice exists, and yes, they're legitimate."

Prove That You Can Practice

Once we've proven your practice exists, the next step is demonstrating you're qualified to provide therapeutic services. AI systems actively look for verifiable evidence of professional credentials and authority.

🎓

Professional Credentials

Verifiable proof of licensure and training

We help structure your bio page to prominently feature links to state license verification pages, certifications, specialized training credentials, and your NPI number. These aren't just for visitors. They're machine-readable signals that AI uses to verify your qualifications.

🏫

Education and Training

Linked to verifiable institutions

We include verifiable information about your degrees, the institutions you attended, and specialized training programs, all with links back to university pages or accreditation bodies. This gives AI a clear chain of verification from your bio page to trusted educational sources.

🤝

Professional Associations

Membership in recognized organizations

Memberships in organizations like APA, NASW, AAMFT, or specialty associations provide third-party validation of your professional standing. We display these with links to your profiles on those platforms, creating yet another verifiable connection that AI can follow.

🎤

Media and Publications

Third-party validation of expertise

If you've been featured in media, published articles, appeared on podcasts, or spoken at events, we showcase these on your bio page. Each external mention is a trust signal that reinforces your authority in the eyes of both potential clients and AI systems.
