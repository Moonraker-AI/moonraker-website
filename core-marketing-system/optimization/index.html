Part 1

Make your services clear

A therapy website can be thoughtful and still be hard to follow. When the question an AI tool asks is "What services do you provide?", a vague website leads to vague or missing answers. We turn unclear pages into clear service, bio, location, and FAQ content so people and search tools know what you offer.

🧩

Service Pages

Clear pages for the specialties you want to be known for

We build clear service pages for the issues, people, and therapy types you want to be known for. Each page explains what you help with, who you help, and how the service works.

Search tools need clear service pages before they can match your practice to the right client need. When the page is specific, both prospective clients and AI tools can understand the offer.

What this covers

  • A dedicated page for each specialty you want to be found for
  • Plain-language descriptions of what you treat and who you help
  • How the service works and what a client can expect
  • A clear next step toward booking a consultation
👤

Bio Pages

Verifiable credentials and NPI links in one place

We build dedicated bio pages so each provider is easy to understand and easy to verify. Credentials, specialized training, and NPI links are structured so visitors and search tools can confirm who provides the care.

A clear bio page also helps connect the provider to the services and locations they work in, so the practice reads as one consistent picture.

📍

Location and Telehealth Pages

Where the practice serves clients

We build location pages that show where the practice serves clients and which areas matter most, including telehealth service areas where that applies.

Local search depends on clear location and service-area information. When the areas you serve are spelled out, it is easier for nearby clients to find you.

❓

Service FAQs

Answers to the questions clients actually ask

We add service-specific FAQ sections that answer the common questions prospective clients have before they reach out, written in plain language.

Helpful FAQs give Google and AI tools clear facts to draw from, and they reduce the friction between a question and a booked consultation.

Why does this matter?

When someone asks Google or an AI tool what a therapist treats, your website is the source those systems read. People cannot find a service Google does not understand, so a vague page leads to vague or missing answers. Clear service, bio, location, and FAQ content helps the right people reach you.

Part 2

Make the site easy to read and use

Once the content is clear, the next step is making the site itself easy for search engines to read and easy for visitors to act on. This is the behind-the-scenes cleanup that helps Google and AI tools understand the facts on each page.

🧮

Search-friendly Page Structure

Titles, headings, internal links, and search labels

We organize page titles, headings, internal links, and behind-the-scenes labels so search engines can read the site clearly. Clear labels help Google and AI tools understand the facts on the page.

What this covers

  • Helpful page titles and a clear heading order
  • Internal links that connect related pages
  • Behind-the-scenes labels that connect pages to the practice, providers, services, and locations
  • A cleaner overall page flow and site path
⚙

Website Cleanup and Speed

The basics that help search engines read the site

We clean up the basics that get in the way of a clear site: headings, page titles, internal links, page structure, and loading speed.

We focus this work where it strengthens your online presence, and avoid busywork that looks active but does not make the practice easier to find, understand, or contact.

How this works

  • Headings and page titles tidied for clarity
  • Internal links and page structure cleaned up
  • Loading speed improved where it helps
  • A clear booking route and contact path so visitors know the next step
