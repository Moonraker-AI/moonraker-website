Pillar 1: Credibility

Prove That You Exist and Are Qualified

The question AI asks

"Does your practice actually exist online?"

Before Google or AI tools trust your practice, they need proof that you are real, located where you say you are, and described the same way across the web.

How we establish your identity

We connect the public proof around your practice: your website, Google Business Profile, local listings, credentials, practice details, and other trusted sources.

  • Domain email and website records where they are useful
  • Local listings and major data sources
  • Google Business Profile improvements and consistent practice details
  • A practice proof hub that connects important trust points
  • Professional credentials, education, associations, and media linked on bio pages

Pillar 2: Optimization

Teach AI About Your Services

The question AI asks

"What services do you provide?"

Google and AI tools need clear information about what you treat, how you help, and where you can see clients. A vague website leads to vague or missing answers.

How we clarify your services

We make your services, locations, FAQs, credentials, and next steps easier to understand for both prospective clients and search tools.

  • Clear service pages for the specialties you want to be known for
  • Search-friendly page labels behind the scenes
  • Dedicated bio pages with verifiable credentials and NPI links
  • Location pages and service-specific FAQ sections
  • Website cleanup: headings, links, page structure, and load speed

Pillar 3: Reputation

Build Ethical Third-party Trust

The question AI asks

"Can the outside world confirm you are trusted?"

Reviews are only one piece of trust. Therapy practices also need ethical third-party proof, partner proof, press, video, and consistent public information.

How we build your authority

We help collect, organize, and connect public proof so Google, AI tools, and prospective clients can better understand why the practice is trustworthy.

  • Endorsement collection forms and recommendations for ethical use
  • Public mentions and image placements where they make sense
  • YouTube channel improvements after client manager access
  • Approved Quora proof content using client-created access
  • Connecting active, practice-facing profiles where they support practice clarity

Pillar 4: Engagement

Guide Visitors Towards a Call

The question AI asks

"Can someone easily connect with them?"

Visibility without conversion is wasted effort. A buried contact form or confusing booking flow makes the practice harder to contact. Clear calls to action and clean conversion paths help prospective clients move from discovery to consultation.

How we help visitors take the next step

We improve hero sections, contact pages, booking flow, and reporting so growing visibility can turn into booked consultations.

  • Homepage hero and service-page CTA improvements
  • Booking flow clarity and contact page structure
  • Reporting for inquiries, appointments, and where they likely came from
  • Improvements to the pages where people decide to reach out
