Pillar 1: Credibility

Prove That You Exist and Are Qualified

The question AI asks

"Does this practice actually exist?"

Millions of new pages appear online every day, most generated by bots. Before Gemini, ChatGPT, or Google will recommend your practice, they need proof that you're real, located where you say you are, and that there's genuine human activity behind your website.

How we establish your identity

We ground your practice to the real world through verified signals that bots simply cannot fake. Every platform we touch and every record we configure gives AI another data point confirming you're a legitimate, credentialed professional.

  • Google Workspace and DNS records (DKIM, DMARC) for domain verification
  • 15 directory citations + 5 major data aggregators
  • Interconnected social profiles across up to 9 platforms (Rising Tide Strategy)
  • Entity Veracity Hub with cryptographic grounding (DID, KML)
  • Professional credentials, education, associations, and media linked on bio pages

Pillar 2: Optimization

Teach AI About Your Services

The question AI asks

"What services do they provide?"

AI needs explicit, structured information about what you treat, the modalities you use, and where you're licensed to practice. A vague website with no clear service pages leads to vague (or zero) recommendations from Google and AI platforms.

How we clarify your services

We make it crystal clear what you treat, how you treat it, and where it happens. Custom service pages, structured data, and technical optimization give AI the clarity it needs to recommend you with confidence.

  • 5 custom service pages with targeted HTML for your top specialties
  • Schema markup (LocalBusiness, FAQPage, Service, Person)
  • Dedicated bio pages with verifiable credentials and NPI links
  • Location pages and service-specific FAQ sections
  • Technical SEO: heading hierarchy, clean structure, instant.page
