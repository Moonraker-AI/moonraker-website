Pillar 4 of 4

Turn visibility into booked consultations.

Being found is only half the work. Once people reach your website, they need an obvious next step. This pillar helps visitors move from discovery to a booked consultation, and helps you see where those inquiries are coming from.

Part 1

Make the next step obvious

Visibility without conversion is wasted effort. A buried contact form or a confusing booking flow makes the practice harder to reach. We work on the pages where people decide to get in touch so the path from discovery to consultation is clear.

👉

Calls to Action

Homepage hero and service-page CTAs

We improve the homepage hero and the calls to action on your service pages so the most important next step is easy to see and easy to act on.

The goal is simple: a visitor who is ready to reach out should never have to hunt for how to do it.

✉

Contact Page Structure

A clear, uncluttered way to reach you

A buried or cluttered contact page costs you inquiries. We structure the contact page so the most useful ways to reach the practice are clear and easy to find.

📅

Booking Flow Clarity

Fewer steps between interest and a consultation

A confusing booking flow loses people who were ready to schedule. We work to make the path to a consultation clearer, so an interested visitor can take the next step without friction.

We work inside the booking tools and accounts you already own. You keep ownership of those accounts; we improve how they are presented on your site.

📝

Conversion-focused Page Structure

Improving the pages where people decide to reach out

We focus on the pages where people decide to reach out, and on the proof visitors look for before they contact you, so those pages do a better job of turning interest into an inquiry.

What this can include

  • Clearer headlines and next steps on key landing pages
  • Service and location pages that answer the obvious questions
  • Trust points and proof placed where they support the decision to contact you
  • A consistent, visible call to action across the pages that matter most

Why does this matter?

When someone asks Google or an AI tool for therapist options, getting them to your website is only the start. If the next step is unclear, that visibility does not turn into booked consultations. Engagement is where growing visibility becomes real inquiries.

Part 2

Measure what is working

You should be able to see whether the work is paying off. We set up practical reporting so you can follow inquiries and appointments over time, and get a sense of where they likely came from, then we recommend where to focus next.

📊

Reporting and Attribution

Inquiries, appointments, and where they likely came from

We set a reporting baseline so you can follow inquiries and appointments, and get a sense of where they likely came from, such as organic search, Maps, or AI tools.

This reporting is about visibility and inquiries over time. It shows where attention is coming from to help guide the work. It is not a promise of a specific number of new clients.

What we track

  • Inquiries and appointment requests over time
  • Where visits and inquiries likely came from
  • How visibility is trending across Google, Maps, organic search, and AI tools
  • Progress against the foundation we set in the first 90 days
🧭

Recommendations and Next Steps

Where to focus next, based on what the reporting shows

Reporting is only useful if it leads to action. As part of ongoing CORE support, we use what the reporting shows to recommend the next improvements, then keep working on the website, proof, and visibility.

Start with what is true, build what is missing, and keep going where it can grow. The reporting tells us where that next bit of growth is most likely to come from.
